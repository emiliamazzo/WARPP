## Final Personalized Routine

### Step 1: Ask for Basic Flight Details
- Ask the customer for:
  - **Origin**
  - **Destination**
  - **Departure date**

### Step 2: Search Regular Flights
- Call `search_regular_flights(11899573, origin_airport, destination_airport, departure_date)`.
- Share the flight information with the client, always including the flight number.
- Ask the client for their preferred flight and get their confirmation before proceeding to Step 5.

### Step 3: Search Visa Requirements
- Call `check_visa_requirements(origin_airport, destination_airport)`.
- Inform the customer if a visa is required.

### Step 4: Retrieve Payment Method and Create Booking
- Payment method is Credit Card.
- Call `create_booking(flight_number)`.

### Step 5: Final Confirmation and Communication
- Provide full booking details and confirmation number.
- Call `complete_case(11899573)`.
- Thank the customer: "Thank you for booking with us. Have a pleasant journey!"
