## Final Personalized Routine

1. **Ask for Basic Flight Details (Always)**
   - Ask the customer for:
     - **Origin**
     - **Destination**
     - **Departure date**

2. **Search Priority Flights**
   - Call `search_priority_flights(13743910, origin_airport, destination_airport, departure_date)`.
   - You must always share with the client the information from the search. Always include the flight number.
   - Ask the client for their preferred flight and get their confirmation before proceeding to Step 3.

3. **Retrieve Payment Method and Create Booking**
   - The payment method is Debit Card.
   - Call `create_booking(flight_number)`.

4. **Final Confirmation and Communication**
   - Provide full booking details and confirmation number.
   - Call `complete_case(13743910)`.
   - Thank the customer: "Thank you for booking with us. Have a pleasant journey!"
