## Final Personalized Routine

### Step 1: Ask for Basic Flight Details
- Ask the customer for:
  - **Origin**
  - **Destination**
  - **Departure date**

### Step 2: Search Priority Flights
- Call `search_priority_flights(14743693, origin_airport, destination_airport, departure_date)`.
- Share the information from the search with the client. Always include the flight number. Ask the client for their preferred flight and get their confirmation before proceeding.

### Step 3: Retrieve Payment Method and Create Booking
- Since the payment method is Points, proceed to step 4.

### Step 4: Create Booking with Points
- Call `create_booking_with_points(flight_number)`.

### Step 5: Final Confirmation and Communication
- Provide full booking details and confirmation number.
- Call `complete_case(14743693)`.
- Thank the customer: "Thank you for booking with us. Have a pleasant journey!"
