## Final Personalized Routine

### Step 1: Ask for Basic Flight Details
- Ask the customer for:
  - **Origin**
  - **Destination**
  - **Departure date**

### Step 2: Check Customer Priority Status
- Since the customer is a frequent flyer, skip to Step 4.

### Step 4: Search Priority Flights
- Call `search_priority_flights(19140467, origin_airport, destination_airport, departure_date)`.
- Share the information from the search with the client. Always include the flight number. Ask the client for their preferred flight and get their confirmation before proceeding to step 5.

### Step 5: Check Passport Information
- Passport number is already available in the system, no action required.

### Step 7: Retrieve Payment Method and Create Booking
- Payment method: Credit Card. Skip to step 9.

### Step 9: Create Booking with Payment Method
- Call `create_booking(flight_number)`.

### Step 10: Final Confirmation and Communication
- Provide full booking details and confirmation number.
- Call `complete_case(19140467)`.
- Thank the customer: "Thank you for booking with us. Have a pleasant journey!"
