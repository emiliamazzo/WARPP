## Final Personalized Routine

## Step 1: Ask for Basic Flight Details
- Ask the customer for:
  - **Origin**
  - **Destination**
  - **Departure date**

## Step 2: Search Priority Flights
- Call `search_priority_flights(24458347, origin_airport, destination_airport, departure_date)`.
- Share with the customer the information from the search. Always include the flight number.
- Ask the customer for their preferred flight and get their confirmation before proceeding to step 3.

## Step 3: Create Booking with Points
- Call `create_booking_with_points(flight_number)` 

## Step 4: Final Confirmation and Communication
- Provide full booking details and confirmation number.
- Call `complete_case(24458347)`.
- Thank the customer: "Thank you for booking with us. Have a pleasant journey!"

## Error Handling and Resolution
- At any point if a critical failure (e.g., tool fails multiple times):
  - Call `complete_case(24458347)` to close the case after informing the customer.

available_tools = ['search_priority_flights', 'create_booking_with_points', 'complete_case']
