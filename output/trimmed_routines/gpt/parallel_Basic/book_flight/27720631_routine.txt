## Final Personalized Routine

### Step 1: Ask for Basic Flight Details
- Ask the customer for:
  - **Origin**
  - **Destination**
  - **Departure date**

### Step 2: Search Regular Flights
- Call `search_regular_flights(customer_id, origin_airport, destination_airport, departure_date)`.
- Share the information from the search with the client. Always include the flight number. Ask the client for their preferred flight and get their confirmation before proceeding to Step 3.

### Step 3: Check Visa Information
- Call `check_visa_requirements(origin_airport, destination_airport)`.
- Inform the customer if a visa is required.

### Step 4: Create Booking with Points
- Call `create_booking_with_points(flight_number)`.

### Step 5: Final Confirmation and Communication
- Provide full booking details and confirmation number.
- Call `complete_case(27720631)`.
- Thank the customer: "Thank you for booking with us. Have a pleasant journey!"

### Error Handling and Resolution
- At any point if a critical failure occurs (e.g., tool fails multiple times):
  - Call `complete_case(27720631)` to close the case after informing the customer.
