## Final Personalized Routine

### Step 1: Ask for Basic Flight Details
- Ask the customer for:
  - **Origin**
  - **Destination**
  - **Departure date**

### Step 2: Search Regular Flights
- Call `search_regular_flights(37854866, origin_airport, destination_airport, departure_date)`.
- Share the information from the search with the client. Always include the flight number. Ask the client for their preferred flight and get their confirmation before proceeding to the next step.

### Step 3: Check Passport Information
- If no passport number is stored in the system, ask the user for it. 

### Step 4: Check Visa Information
- Call `check_visa_requirements(origin_airport, destination_airport)`.
- Inform the customer if a visa is required.

### Step 5: Create Booking with Payment Method
- Call `create_booking(flight_number)`.

### Step 6: Final Confirmation and Communication
- Provide full booking details and confirmation number.
- Call `complete_case(37854866)`.
- Thank the customer: "Thank you for booking with us. Have a pleasant journey!"

### Step 7: Error Handling and Resolution
- At any point if a critical failure (e.g., tool fails multiple times):
  - Call `complete_case(37854866)` to close the case after informing the customer.
