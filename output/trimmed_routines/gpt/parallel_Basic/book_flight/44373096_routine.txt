  - **Destination**
  - **Departure date**

- Call `search_priority_flights(44373096, origin_airport, destination_airport, departure_date)`.
- Share the information from the search with the customer, including the flight number.
- Ask the client for their preferred flight and get their confirmation before proceeding to the next step.

### Step 3: Check Passport Information
- Confirm the passport information: P8901234.

### Step 4: Retrieve Payment Method and Create Booking
- The payment method is Debit Card. Proceed to create booking with this method.

### Step 5: Create Booking with Payment Method
- Call `create_booking(flight_number)`.

### Step 6: Final Confirmation and Communication
- Provide full booking details and confirmation number.
- Call `complete_case(44373096)`.
- Thank the customer: "Thank you for booking with us. Have a pleasant journey!"

### Error Handling and Resolution
- At any point if a critical failure occurs (e.g., tool fails multiple times):
  - Call `complete_case(44373096)` to close the case after informing the customer.
