## Final Personalized Routine:

### Step 1: Ask for Basic Flight Details
- Ask the customer for:
  - **Origin**
  - **Destination**
  - **Departure date**

### Step 2: Search Priority Flights
- Call `search_priority_flights(53513248, origin_airport, destination_airport, departure_date)`.
- Relay the search results to the customer, ensuring to include the flight number.
- Confirm the customer's preferred flight choice before proceeding to the next step.

### Step 3: Check Passport Information
- Client's passport number is on file. Proceed without additional passport checks.

### Step 4: Retrieve Payment Method and Create Booking
- Customer will use Points for booking. Go to the next step.

### Step 5: Create Booking with Points
- Call `create_booking_with_points(flight_number)`

### Step 6: Final Confirmation and Communication
- Provide full booking details along with the confirmation number.
- Call `complete_case(53513248)`.
- Thank the customer: "Thank you for booking with us. Have a pleasant journey!"

### Error Handling and Resolution
- At any point, if a critical failure occurs (e.g., tool fails multiple times):
  - Call `complete_case(53513248)` to close the case after informing the customer.
