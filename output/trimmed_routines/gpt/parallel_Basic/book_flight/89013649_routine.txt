## Final Personalized Routine

1. **Step 1: Ask for Basic Flight Details**
   - Ask the customer for:
     - **Origin**
     - **Destination**
     - **Departure date**

2. **Step 2: Search Regular Flights**
   - Call `search_regular_flights(89013649, origin_airport, destination_airport, departure_date)`.
   - You must always share with the client the information from the search. Always include the flight number. Ask the client for their preferred flight and get their confirmation before proceeding to the next step.

3. **Step 3: Check Visa Information**
   - Call `check_visa_requirements(origin_airport, destination_airport)`.
   - Inform customer if visa is required.

4. **Step 4: Create Booking with Payment Method**
   - Call `create_booking(flight_number)`.

5. **Step 5: Final Confirmation and Communication**
   - Provide full booking details and confirmation number.
   - Call `complete_case(89013649)`.
   - Thank the customer: "Thank you for booking with us. Have a pleasant journey!"
