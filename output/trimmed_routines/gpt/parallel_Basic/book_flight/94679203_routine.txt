## Final Personalized Routine

### Step 1: Ask for Basic Flight Details
- Ask the customer for:
  - **Origin**
  - **Destination**
  - **Departure date**

### Step 2: Search Priority Flights
- Call `search_priority_flights(94679203, origin_airport, destination_airport, departure_date)`.
- Share all flight information with the client, including flight number.
- Ask the client for their preferred flight and get confirmation before proceeding to Step 3.

### Step 3: Retrieve Payment Method and Create Booking
- The payment method is **Credit Card**.
  - Call `create_booking(flight_number)`.

### Step 4: Final Confirmation and Communication
- Provide full booking details and confirmation number.
- Call `complete_case(94679203)`.
- Thank the customer: "Thank you for booking with us. Have a pleasant journey!"
