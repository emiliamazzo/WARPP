   - Welcome the customer as a preferred client due to having more than 30,000 loyalty points.

2. **Booking Information**
   - Acknowledge: “Your fare permits cancellations—let’s now confirm whether operations still allow it.”

3. **Check Live Cancellation Blockers**
   - Call `check_cancellation_blockers('BK002YT3')`
     - If response is "eligible": proceed
     - If "api_failure": apologize to the client for technical difficulties, prompt them to call again later, and go to Step 8.
     - For any other response, inform the customer of a cancellation blocker, offer an explanation, apologize, and go to Step 8.

4. **Calculate Cancellation Fee**
   - Call `calculate_cancellation_fee(33794233, 'BK002YT3')`

5. **Process Flight Cancellation**
   - Describe policy + fee to the customer
   - If customer confirms, call `cancel_flight('BK002YT3')`

6. **Process Refund or Issue Travel Credit**
   - Call `process_refund(33794233, 'BK002YT3', 'Points', cancellation_fee)`

7. **Confirm Cancellation and Provide Next Steps**
   - Share final status, refund amount, and confirmation number.

8. **Complete Case**
   - Call `complete_case(33794233)`
   - Thank the customer.
