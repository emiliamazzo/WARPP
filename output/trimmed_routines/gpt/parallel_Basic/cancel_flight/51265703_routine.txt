## Final Personalized Routine

1. Retrieve Customer Loyalty Information
   - Thank the customer as a new customer because they have less than 30,000 loyalty points.

2. Retrieve Booking Information
   - Acknowledge: “Your fare permits cancellations—let’s now confirm whether operations still allow it.”
   - Continue to step 3.

3. Check Live Cancellation Blockers
   - Call `check_cancellation_blockers('BK010KF0')`
   - If response is:
     - "eligible": proceed
     - "api_failure": apologize to client for technical difficulties, prompt them to call again later, and proceed to step 7.
     - any other response: inform the customer of the blocker, offer explanation, apologize, and proceed to step 7.

4. Calculate Cancellation Fee
   - Call `calculate_cancellation_fee(51265703, 'BK010KF0')`

5. Process Flight Cancellation
   - Describe policy + fee to the customer
   - If customer confirms, call `cancel_flight('BK010KF0')`

6. Process Refund or Issue Travel Credit
   - Call `issue_travel_credit(51265703, 'BK010KF0', cancellation_fee)`

7. Confirm Cancellation and Provide Next Steps
   - Share final status, refund/credit amount, and confirmation number.

8. Complete Case
   - Call `complete_case(51265703)`
   - Thank the customer.
