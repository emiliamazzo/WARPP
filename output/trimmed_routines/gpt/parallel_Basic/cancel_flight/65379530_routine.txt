## Final Personalized Routine

1. Retrieve Customer Loyalty Information  
   - Thank them as a new customer.

2. Retrieve Booking Information  
   - "Your fare permits cancellations—let’s now confirm whether operations still allow it."

3. Check Live Cancellation Blockers  
   a. Call `check_cancellation_blockers('BK010KF0')` to see if there are any blockers that prevent the cancellation.  
   b. If response is:
      - "eligible": proceed
      - "api_failure": apologize to the client for the technical difficulties, prompt them to call again at a later time, and proceed to step 7.
      - any other response: inform the customer that there is a blocker that prevents them from cancelling the flight at this time. Offer and explanation, apologize, and proceed to step 7.

4. Calculate Cancellation Fee  
   a. Call `calculate_cancellation_fee(65379530, 'BK010KF0')`

5. Process Flight Cancellation  
   a. Describe policy + fee to the customer  
   b. If customer confirms, call `cancel_flight('BK010KF0')`

6. Process Refund or Issue Travel Credit  
   a. Call `process_refund(65379530, 'BK010KF0', 'Debit Card', cancellation_fee)`

7. Complete Case  
   a. Call `complete_case(65379530)`  
   b. Thank the customer.
