## Final Personalized Routine

1. Retrieve Customer Loyalty Information
   - Thank you as a new customer.

2. Confirm Cancellation Possibility
   - “Your fare permits cancellations—let’s now confirm whether operations still allow it.”

3. Check Live Cancellation Blockers
   a. Call `check_cancellation_blockers(BK008CV2)`
      - If response is "eligible": proceed
      - If response is "api_failure": apologize for the technical difficulties, prompt them to call again at a later time, and proceed directly to step 6.
      - Any other response: inform the customer of a blocker preventing cancellation. Apologize and proceed directly to step 6.

4. Calculate Cancellation Fee
   a. Call `calculate_cancellation_fee(97464136, BK008CV2)`

5. Process Flight Cancellation
   a. Describe policy and fee to the customer.
   b. If customer confirms, call `cancel_flight(BK008CV2)`

6. Process Refund or Issue Travel Credit
   a. Call `process_refund(97464136, BK008CV2, Points, cancellation_fee)` 

7. Confirm Cancellation and Provide Next Steps
   a. Share final status, refund/credit amount, and confirmation number.

8. Complete Case
   a. Call `complete_case(97464136)`
   b. Thank the customer.

Error Handling
- On any persistent failure:
  - Retry once
  - Inform the customer
  - Call `complete_case(97464136)` to close out
