## Final Personalized Routine

1. Retrieve Customer Loyalty Information
   - You have more than 30,000 loyalty points: Welcome as a preferred client.

2. Retrieve Booking Information
   - Your fare permits cancellations—let’s now confirm whether operations still allow it.

3. Check Live Cancellation Blockers
   - Call `check_cancellation_blockers('BK001XZ9')`
     - If response is:
       - "eligible": proceed
       - "api_failure": apologize to the client for the technical difficulties, prompt them to call again at a later time, and proceed directly to step 8.
       - any other response: inform the customer that there is a blocker that prevents them from cancelling the flight at this time. Offer an explanation, apologize, and proceed directly to step 8.

4. Calculate Cancellation Fee
   - Call `calculate_cancellation_fee(97501811, 'BK001XZ9')`

5. Process Flight Cancellation
   - Describe policy + fee to the customer
   - If the customer confirms, call `cancel_flight('BK001XZ9')`

6. Process Refund or Issue Travel Credit
   - Since refundable or insurance was purchased for this booking:
     - Call `process_refund(97501811, 'BK001XZ9', 'Debit Card', cancellation_fee)`

7. Confirm Cancellation and Provide Next Steps
   - Share final status, refund amount, and confirmation number.

8. Complete Case
   - Call `complete_case(97501811)`
   - Thank the customer.

Error Handling
- On any persistent failure:
  - Retry once
  - Inform the customer
  - Call `complete_case(97501811)` to close out
