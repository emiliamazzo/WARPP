## Final Personalized Routine

1. Check Account Status  
   - The account is delinquent; note it for collections and continue.

2. Assess Payment Urgency  
   - Your payment is 9 days overdue. Let’s get you back on track.

3. Financial Assistance & Tax Calculation
   - Let me check if your insurance can cover part of this payment.
   - Call `get_insurance_payment_portion(14075852, 'Cignexa')`
     - If approved, say "Great news! Your insurance will cover part of your claim."
     - If declined, say "No additional assistance is available at this time."

4. Suggest Late-Fee Waivers  
   - Call `calculate_late_fee_waiver_eligibility_extra(14075852)`.
     - If eligible, ask “We can waive $15 in late fees. Shall I apply that for you?”
     - If yes, call `apply_fee_waiver(14075852, 15)`.
     - If no, proceed.

5. Always Calculate Final Amount Due  
   - Call `calculate_patient_responsibility(14075852, 'Cignexa')`
   - Say “Your total due today is $X. Would you like to proceed with payment?”
     - If no, say “No problem; let me know if you need anything else,” and call `complete_case(14075852)`.
     - If yes, continue.

6. Always Ask Client if They Want to Pay in USD or Another Currency  
   - Ask “Would you like to pay in another currency?”
     - If yes, get the desired currency, call `currency_exchange(patient_responsibility_amount, from_currency='USD', to_currency=preferred_currency)`, and say “That’s [convertedAmount] in [currency].”
     - If no, continue in USD.

7. Process the Payment  
   - Explain “ACH transfers take 3–5 business days to clear.”
   - Call `initiate_ach_transaction(14075852, patient_responsibility_amount)`.
     - If `initiate_ach_transaction` returns transient error, prompt the client to call 1-800-555-BILL to continue the request and call `complete_case(14075852)`.

8. Send receipt  
   - Call `issue_receipt(14075852, patient_responsibility_amount)` to send an email confirmation. Inform the user that a receipt has been sent to them.

9. Offer Payment Plan
   - Ask: "Would you like to set up a payment plan?"
     - If yes, ask how much to set for the monthly amount → call `setup_payment_plan(monthly_amount)`.
     - If no, proceed.

10. Finalize
    - Provide confirmation number.
    - Call `complete_case(14075852)`.
