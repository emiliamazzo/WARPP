**Final Personalized Routine**

1. Confirm Outstanding Balance
   - "It looks like you have a balance to pay today. Let's get that done!"

2. Assess Payment Urgency
   - "Your payment is 7 days overdue. Let’s get you back on track."

3. Financial & Insurance Assistance
   - "Let me check if your insurance can cover part of this payment."
   - Call `get_insurance_payment_portion(47690673, 'Humena')`
   - If approved, say "Great news! Your insurance will cover part of your claim."
   - If declined, say "No additional assistance is available at this time."

4. Calculate Final Amount Due
   - Call `calculate_patient_responsibility(47690673, 'Humena')`
   - "Your total due today is $X. Would you like to proceed with payment?"
     - If no, say “No problem; let me know if you need anything else,” and call `complete_case(47690673)`
     - If yes, continue to Step 5.

5. Currency Preference
   - "Would you like to pay in another currency?"
     - If yes, get preferred currency, call `currency_exchange(patient_responsibility_amount, from_currency='USD', to_currency=preferred_currency)`, and say “That’s [convertedAmount] in [currency].”
     - If no, continue in USD.

6. Process Payment via Credit Card
   - Call `run_fraud_check(47690673, patient_responsibility_amount)`
   - If flagged, say “We need to review this transaction—please call our billing hotline”. Call `get_hospital_contact_info(550407)` to share the hotline number and call `complete_case(47690673)`
   - If not flagged, call `initiate_3ds_auth(47690673)`
     - If initiate_3ds_auth fails, prompt the client to call 1-800-555-BILL to continue the request, then call `complete_case(47690673)`
   - Call `process_payment(47690673, patient_responsibility_amount)`

7. Send Receipt
   - Call `issue_receipt(47690673, patient_responsibility_amount)`
   - Inform the user a receipt has been sent.

8. Finalize
   - Provide confirmation number
   - Call `complete_case(47690673)`
