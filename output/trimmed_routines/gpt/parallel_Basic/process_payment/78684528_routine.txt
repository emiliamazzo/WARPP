1. Provide Status & Balance Information
    - The account is delinquent, and the balance is $2302. Let's get that payment done!

2. Check Payment Urgency
    - Urgency is low. Simply acknowledge and proceed.

3. Financial Assistance Evaluation
    - Inform the user: "Let me check if your insurance can cover part of this payment."
    - Call `get_insurance_payment_portion(customer_id, 'Humena')`.
    - If approved, say "Great news! Your insurance will cover part of your claim."
    - If declined, say "No additional assistance is available at this time."

4. Calculate Final Amount Due
    - Call `calculate_patient_responsibility(78684528, 'Humena')`.
    - Say “Your total due today is $X. Would you like to proceed with payment?”
      - If no, say “No problem; let me know if you need anything else,” and call `complete_case(78684528)`.
      - If yes, continue.

5. Offer Currency Choice
    - Ask “Would you like to pay in another currency?”
      - If yes, get the desired currency, call `currency_exchange(patient_responsibility_amount, from_currency='USD', to_currency=preferred_currency)`, and say “That’s [convertedAmount] in [currency].”.
      - If no, continue in USD.

6. Process Payment via Bank Transfer
    - Explain “ACH transfers take 3–5 business days to clear.”
    - Call `initiate_ach_transaction(78684528, patient_responsibility_amount)`.
      - If initiate_ach_transaction returns transient error, prompt the client to call 1-800-555-BILL to continue the request and call `complete_case(78684528)`.

7. Send Receipt
    - Call `issue_receipt(78684528, patient_responsibility_amount)`.
    - Inform the user that a receipt has been sent to them.

8. Offer Payment Plan
    - Ask: "Would you like to set up a payment plan?"
      - If yes: ask how much to set for the monthly amount and call `setup_payment_plan(monthly_amount)`.
    
9. Finalize
    - Provide confirmation number.
    - Call `complete_case(78684528)`.
