## Final Personalized Routine

1. Collect Withdrawal Amount
   - Ask the customer how much they would like to withdraw

2. Process Withdrawal
   - Call `process_retirement_withdrawal(13783151, withdrawal_amount)`
   - Confirm success and share the final amount with the customer

3. Complete Case
   - Call `complete_case(13783151)`
   - Provide a confirmation message to the customer

4. Error Handling
   - If any step fails:
     - Retry once
     - If still failing, inform the customer and close the case
