## Final Personalized Routine

1. **Check Withdrawal Eligibility**
   - The customer is eligible for withdrawal.

2. **Collect Withdrawal Amount**
   - Ask the customer how much they would like to withdraw.

3. **Process Withdrawal**
   - Call `process_retirement_withdrawal(16881362, withdrawal_amount)`
   - If success:
     - Confirm success and share final amount with the customer.
   - If failure:
     - Retry once.
     - If still failing, inform the customer and proceed to complete the case.

4. **Complete Case**
   - Call `complete_case(16881362)`
   - Provide a confirmation message to the customer.
