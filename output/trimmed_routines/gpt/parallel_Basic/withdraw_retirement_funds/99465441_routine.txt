## Final Personalized Routine

1. Collect Withdrawal Amount  
   - Ask the customer how much they would like to withdraw

2. Process Withdrawal
   - Call `process_retirement_withdrawal(99465441, withdrawal_amount)`
   - If success, confirm success and share final amount with customer
   - If failure, retry once
     - If retry fails, inform the customer and close the case by calling `complete_case(99465441)`

3. Complete Case
   - Call `complete_case(99465441)`
   - Provide a confirmation message to the customer
